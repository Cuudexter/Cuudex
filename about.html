Welcome, cuurious visitor!

Cuudex is intended as a stream browsing site for Pupals looking for a Féileacán Cú stream or collab to (re)watch or recommend. Cuu has had no hand in it, it's simply a passion project born from a wish to tag streams to filter them more easily, as well as my own frustration at the lack of various Youtube functionlity. It might also be a not-at-all delayed birthday gift for someold guy and his ancient body roomie... Surely all of November still counts, right...?

The stream tags are subjective opinion, but I try to make them generally acceptable, and will consider suggestions carefully! I try to keep them pretty high-level, so that while most (*cough* all) streams make me laugh, mainly the ones centred around humerous games are tagged "Funny". That's probably my worst choice for a tag, actually... Regarding submitted Tag Suggestions, I have to review them manually due to technical limitations (like the lack of an actual server). I'm sorry if your suggestion doesn't come through, please bear with me, I'm ~juuust a Pupal~

Tragically, graphic design is not my passion. And I'm not actually that good at making websites, but here we are.

Feel free to reach out to me on Discord, username Cuudexter, especially if there's a collab stream I've missed after a few days, or if there's something weird about the tags!

Have a wonderful day, everywan.
